Choose a Class


Thaumaturgist
Experts in the primal forces of Life and Death, Thaumaturgists are well-versed in the cycle of life. They are patient, let their garden bloom, starting weak as a sapling but growing strong as the oak.
Preferred Effects: Healing, Fertility, Rebirth, Transformation, Death, Power

Elementalist
Manipulating the primal Titanic forces of the elements, Elementalists are versatile and impulsive. They can display an incredible array of different powers, with monstrous bursts of energies pulverizing any obstacle.
Preferred Effects: Water, Fire, Earth, Wind, Light, Darkness

Biomancer
Believing that doing a job right means doing it yourself, Biomancers are an aggressive, focused type. Using any means available to improve their abilities, they then use their perfected body to crush their foes.
Preferred Effects: Strength, Toughness, Speed, Intellect, Protection, Weakness

Guru
Reveling in the attention others can't help but give them, Gurus get along with anyone and everyone, carefully planning each social encounter like a general plans for a battle.
Preferred Effects: Luck, Sociality, Seduction, Riches, Beauty, Worship




Yogi
Living true to the adage of "Wellness is Goodness", Yogis bless others - and themselves - with positivity. They always have to be doing something - even if that thing is peaceful meditation.
Preferred Effects: Happiness, Confidence, Peace, Courage, Love, Truth

Hexer
Using the darkest of magics, Hexers eschew the dangers of self-experimentation...by experimenting on others. Voodoo dolls, curses, bad luck and bad omens are their trade.
Preferred Effects: Confusion, Fear, Madness, Anger, Sadness, Illusion

Wyrdseer
Peering into the very Web of Fate, wyrdseers possess the most complex of abilities. Quite often, their strange manneurisms and patterns only reveal their sense much later - to world-shattering effect.
Preferred Effects: Fate, Perception, Dreams, Mystery, Soul, Messages, Travel
